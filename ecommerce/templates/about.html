OUR STORY STARTS HERE

Sleek Man Grooming is a MEN-CARE BRAND under BEALFON ENTERPRISE a sole owned business.
Sleek Man Product line which came about when a Young Vibrant Lady, developed passioned for Empowering Men's Personal Care and providing vital information about facial hair maintenance & growth.



Because Her love for beard is unmatched, She then decided to figure out a way to source for locally made Men's Product.



This venture took a while to yield results as it was a gradual process of testing certain carrier and essential oils to discover the unique formula eventually found today & we are here!



The Company takes pride in originality which drove us towards more Men's Organic Skin Care Products.



Our desire is to create products that are effective for solving Skin and Facial Hair Challenges faced by many African Men.



Our products are carefully selected & tested before they are released in the market for consumption.



In order to get that fine & unique essence, which our customers applaud us for.
